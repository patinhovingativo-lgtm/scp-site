SERVER RULES

All personnel must follow these rules at all times. Violations result in disciplinary action.

NOTICE: These rules apply to both the Discord server and Roblox game. Staff decisions are final. Appeal via ticket only.
§1 — DISCORD SERVER RULES
#RULEPENALTY
01Respect all members. No harassment, personal attacks, or discrimination of any kind.Warning → Ban
02No NSFW content. Sexual, graphic, or violent content in any channel.Instant Ban
03No spam or flooding. Repeated messages, excessive emojis, channel flooding.Warning → Timeout
04No unauthorized advertising. Other servers, groups or services without staff approval.Kick → Ban
05Use correct channels. Keep conversations in appropriate channels. Read descriptions.Warning
06No impersonation. Of staff, O5 Council, The Administrator, or other members.Instant Ban
07English in main channels. For moderation purposes. International channel available.Warning
08No doxxing or threatening. Sharing personal information or threatening harm.Instant Ban
09Respect staff decisions. Do not argue moderation in public. Use tickets.Warning → Mute
10No alt accounts to bypass punishments.Permanent Ban
§2 — ROBLOX GAME RULES
#RULEPENALTY
01No exploiting or cheating. Scripts, exploits, or third-party tools of any kind.Instant Perm. Ban
02Follow chain of command. Obey superior-ranked personnel during operations.Warning → Demotion
03No RDM (Random Deathmatch). Killing without proper justification or ROE.Kick → Ban
04Stay in your division role. Security guards do not conduct research. Researchers do not storm areas.Warning → Demotion
05No unauthorized D-Class termination. Only under approved conditions.Warning → Demotion
06No unauthorized breach of containment for any reason.Demotion → Ban
07Follow Emergency Protocol during declared breaches or lockdowns.Warning
08Uniform standards apply during official operations.Warning
§3 — MILSIM OPERATION RULES
#RULEPENALTY
01Attend committed operations. If you sign up, you attend. Notify CO in advance if you cannot.Warning → Demotion
02Inactivity policy. 14+ days without approved LOA = demotion or removal from division.Demotion
03Request LOA in advance. Submit Leave of Absence via ticket before absence begins.—
04Debrief after operations. All operations must be logged within 24 hours (for relevant roles).Warning
