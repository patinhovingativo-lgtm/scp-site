PATROL GUIDE
This guide defines standard patrol procedures for all Security Department personnel.
1. BEFORE YOUR PATROL

✦ Log in at the armory — receive your assigned weapon and keycard clearance.
✦ Check the briefing board for any active alerts, escaped D-Class, or SCP status changes.
✦ Confirm your patrol route with your patrol leader or Security Commander.
✦ Always patrol in pairs minimum. Solo patrol is only authorized for SC-2+.

2. PATROL ROUTES
ZONEROUTEMIN. RANK
Zone A — Entrance & ProcessingMain gate → Processing bay → Briefing room → Checkpoint AlphaSecurity Cadet
Zone B — D-Class WingD-Class cells → Testing corridors → D-Class cafeteria → Checkpoint BravoJunior Security Officer
Zone C — Containment Level 1Safe-class cells → Monitoring room → Research access → Checkpoint CharlieSecurity Officer
Zone D — Containment Level 2Euclid-class cells → HazMat corridor → Medical access point → Checkpoint DeltaSenior Security Officer
Zone E — Containment Level 3 (KETER)Keter containment → Reinforced corridor → Emergency blast doorsSecurity Inspector (SC-2)
3. DURING PATROL

✦ Check every door seal in your zone. Report anomalous seal readings immediately.
✦ Verify all D-Class are accounted for at each cell block.
✦ Challenge any unescorted personnel in restricted zones. Request ID and clearance badge.
✦ Do NOT engage Keter-class entities alone. Call for backup immediately.
✦ Log patrol start and end time in the Security Log channel on Discord.

4. ENCOUNTERING A BREACH
BREACH PROTOCOL — MEMORIZE THIS:
1. Announce breach on radio: "BREACH IN [ZONE] — [SCP NUMBER OR DESCRIPTION]"
2. Evacuate all civilian personnel (Research, Medical, Psychology) from the zone.
3. Seal blast doors if authorized to do so.
4. Hold position and wait for MTF or DOHE — do NOT pursue Keter alone.
5. Provide situation report every 2 minutes until relief arrives.
5. D-CLASS ESCORT PROCEDURE

✦ D-Class must be cuffed during all transfers outside their wing.
✦ Maintain 2-meter distance — do NOT turn your back to a D-Class.
✦ If D-Class becomes hostile: verbal warning → warning shot → lethal force (requires documentation).
✦ Researcher escorts: you follow researcher instructions within your ROE during tests.

6. AFTER PATROL

✦ Return weapon and keycard to armory. Log return time.
✦ Submit patrol report in Security Discord channel within 1 hour.
✦ Report any anomalous activity, unaccounted D-Class, or structural damage observed.

DEPARTMENT RULES
#RULE
01 D-Class termination requires documented authorization or direct imminent threat justification.
02 Checkpoints must be manned at all times during active operations.
03 Lethal force against Foundation personnel requires O5-level authorization.
04 Security escorts for researchers are mandatory for Euclid-class testing and above.
05 All weapons must be logged in and out at the armory before and after every shift.
06 During a breach, Emergency Protocol takes effect — protect personnel, contain the anomaly.
07 No unauthorized personnel may pass a checkpoint. No exceptions.
08 Inactive members (14+ days without LOA) will be demoted one rank.
