PUBLIC — CODE OF ETHICS
PUBLIC — CODE OF ETHICS SUMMARY
The following are the publicly known ethical standards enforced by the CE across all of Site-07.
§ I — CONDUCT & BEHAVIOR

All personnel must conduct themselves with professionalism at all times. Harassment, discrimination, and abuse of authority are grounds for immediate investigation.

§ II — AUTHORITY & CHAIN OF COMMAND

Orders from superiors must be followed unless they violate this Code. Illegal orders must be reported to CE immediately. Rank does not exempt anyone from ethical standards.

§ III — OPERATIONAL CONDUCT

Use of force must be proportional. Unauthorized experimentation, D-Class abuse, and unsanctioned containment breaches are major violations.

§ IV — INFORMATION & CONFIDENTIALITY

Classified information may not be shared beyond required clearance. Leaking Foundation intelligence is grounds for immediate dismissal.

§ V — ENFORCEMENT & DISCIPLINARY ACTION

Violations are investigated by CE. Punishments range from formal warning to rank reduction to permanent expulsion. The accused has the right to a formal hearing.

🔒 CLASSIFIED — CE INTERNAL INVESTIGATION PROTOCOLS
CLASSIFIED — CE MEMBERS ONLY.
INVESTIGATION PROCEDURE

1. Receive report via secure CE channel. 2. Assign lead investigator (must have no conflict of interest). 3. Collect evidence — interview witnesses separately. 4. Present findings to CE Supervisor. 5. Issue ruling within 72 hours of evidence collection completion.

EVIDENCE STANDARDS

All evidence must be timestamped and sourced. Hearsay is not admissible without corroboration. Discord logs, Roblox recordings, and written witness statements are accepted.

RULING CLASSIFICATIONS

Level 1 — Warning: Minor violation, first offense.
Level 2 — Demotion: Repeated or moderate violation.
Level 3 — Department Removal: Severe or deliberate violation.
Level 4 — Site Ban: Requires O5 confirmation. Reserved for the most severe cases.

CONFIDENTIALITY

Ongoing investigations are classified until a ruling is issued. CE members may not discuss active cases outside of the CE channel.
