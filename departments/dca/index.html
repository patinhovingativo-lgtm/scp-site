🔒 CLASSIFIED — DCA ACTIVE CLASSIFICATION DATABASE
CLASSIFIED — DCA PERSONNEL ONLY.
CLASSIFICATION CRITERIA

SAFE: Anomaly is well-understood, easily contained, poses minimal risk without interaction.
EUCLID: Anomaly requires more resources to contain or is insufficiently understood.
KETER: Anomaly is difficult or impossible to contain consistently. Active threat to personnel.

PENDING CLASSIFICATIONS

All pending classification requests are tracked in the DCA private Discord channel. Analysts are assigned by the Threat Classification Supervisor. Priority cases (active breach) are fast-tracked within 1 hour.

RECLASSIFICATION PROTOCOL

To reclassify an entity: (1) Submit reclassification request with incident evidence. (2) Risk Assessment Specialist reviews. (3) Director approves/denies. (4) Update the SCP database. Keter reclassifications require O5 sign-off.
